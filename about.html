About @AaronDidit

The @AaronDidit brand came from an Instagram account where I wanted to have a platform to share my work with other creative people, it was also to show off my work to people who were interested in the creative space. The plan started off to post my creative work ranging from video to graphci design around three to four days a week. Then the opputunity came up in college to make a website that we could use as a portfolio piece to show off our work. So I decided to build on the @AaronDidit visuals brand and I thought no better way to do that than build a website, make sure to follow us @AaronDidit
